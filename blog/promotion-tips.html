YoursMed App Kya Hai?

YoursMed ek multi-task earning platform hai jisme users daily check-ins, video offers, surveys, word puzzles aur offerwalls jaise tasks karke coins earn karte hain. App ka interface friendly aur gamified hai — jisse user engagement zyada hoti hai.

Key Features

  • Multiple Earning Options: Daily check-ins, video offers, Pubscale offerwall, surveys, word puzzles aur mini-games.
  • High Paying Offerwall: Pubscale offerwall ko app “Highest Paying Offers” ke taur par promote karti hai — jahan kuch tasks 50,000+ coins tak reward dete hain.
  • Gamified Experience: Unlockable features, badges, progress bars aur daily streaks se earning interesting rehti hai.
  • Wallet & Withdrawals: Users apni earned coins wallet mein dekh sakte hain aur supported methods se withdrawal kar sakte hain (app policies ke mutabiq).
