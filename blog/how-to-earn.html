How to Earn from Tasks on YoursMed

Yahan aap seekhenge ke YoursMed par tasks complete karke earning kaise hoti hai.

1. Account banao aur login karo.

2. Daily tasks complete karo jaise post share, like, ya follow.

3. Jab minimum balance poora ho jaye, “Withdraw” section me jaakar paise nikaalo.

How Our Funds System Works (پیسے جمع کرنے اور نکالنے کا طریقہ)

1. Fund Deposit (رقم جمع کروانا)
Deposit sirf un logon ke liye hota hai jo apna product, page, ya video promote karwana chahtay hain.
Example: Agar aap apni online shop, app, ya post viral karwana chahtay hain, to aapko deposit karna hota hai.
Ye funds YoursMed ke task performers ko diye jate hain jo aapki promotion complete karte hain.
Simple words me: Deposit = Advertisement ke liye payment.

2. Withdraw (پیسے نکالنا)
Withdrawal sirf un logon ke liye hai jo platform par tasks perform karke earn karte hain.
Aapko deposit karne ki zarurat nahi.
Aap sirf apne daily tasks complete karo aur balance automatic account me add hota hai.
Minimum withdrawal limit aur schedule rules “Withdrawal Policy” page par diye gaye hain.

3. Safety & Transparency (محفوظ اور شفاف نظام)
Har deposit aur withdrawal transaction securely track hoti hai.
Hum kabhi bhi users se earning karne ke liye deposit nahi mangte.
Har deposit advertising campaign ke liye hi use hota hai, earning ke liye nahi.

FAQs

Q: Kya mujhe earning start karne ke liye deposit karna zaruri hai?
A: Nahi, bilkul nahi. Deposit sirf un logon ke liye hai jo apne products promote karwana chahte hain. Tasks karne ke liye koi deposit required nahi.

Q: Deposit ka paisa kahan jata hai?
A: Ye paisa advertisers ke campaigns par use hota hai, jisse performers ko payment milti hai.

Q: Withdrawal kaise hota hai?
A: Jab aap daily tasks complete karte hain, aapke account me balance add hota hai. Minimum limit puri hone par aap withdraw kar sakte hain.
